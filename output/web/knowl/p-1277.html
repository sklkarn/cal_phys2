Paragraph

A plano-convex lens of radius of curvature 1 m is placed, curved side down, on a flat glass plate and illuminated from above with light of wavelength 450 nm so as to form Newton rings.
  1. Is a bright or a dark spot observed at the center of the rings when viewed from above?
  2. What is the radius of the 4’th bright ring when viewed from above?
  3. What is the radius of the 4’th bright ring when viewed from below?
