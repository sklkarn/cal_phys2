Differentiating eqn. (5.3.12) w.r.t. \('i'\) and set that to zero, we get -
\begin{equation} \left.\frac{\,\partial \phi}{\partial i}\right]_{max}= 0 \tag{5.3.13} \end{equation}
\begin{equation*} \text{now,}\quad \frac{\,\partial \phi}{\partial i} = 4\left[\frac{\,\partial}{\,\partial i}\left\{\sin^{-1}\left(\frac{\sin i}{\eta}\right)\right\}\right]-2 =0 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,} \quad 4\left[\frac{\,\partial}{\,\partial u}\left(\sin^{-1}u\right)\frac{\,\partial u}{\,\partial i }\right]-2 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{where,} \quad u = \frac{\sin i}{\eta} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \Rightarrow\quad \frac{\,\partial u}{\,\partial i } = \frac{\cos i}{\eta} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{and} \quad \frac{\,\partial}{\,\partial u }\left(\sin^{-1}u\right)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-u^{2}}} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad 4\left[\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{\sin^{2}i}{\eta^{2}}}}\cdot \frac{\cos i}{\eta}\right]-2 =0 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad 4\left[\frac{\eta}{\sqrt{\eta^{2}-\sin^{2}i}}\cdot \frac{\cos i}{\eta}\right]-2 =0 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad 2\cos i= \sqrt{\eta^{2}-\sin^{2}i} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad 4\cos^{2} i = \eta^{2}-(1-\cos^{2}i) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation} \text{or,}\quad \cos^{2} i = \frac{\eta^{2}-1}{3} \tag{5.3.14} \end{equation}
Since \(\eta =1.331\) for red light, angle of incidence \(i\) at maximum deviation is given by
\begin{equation*} \left. i\right]_{\phi_{max}}=59.53^{o}, \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \left. r\right]_{\phi_{max}}=40.36^{o}, \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{and}\quad \left. \phi\right]_{{max}}=42.37^{o} \end{equation*}
Also \(\eta =1.343\) for violet light,
\begin{equation*} \left. i\right]_{\phi_{max}}=58.83^{o}, \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \left. r\right]_{\phi_{max}}=39.58^{o}, \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{and}\quad \left. \phi\right]_{{max}}=40.65^{o} \end{equation*}
