The relative error in Z is
\begin{equation*} \frac{\Delta Z}{Z} = 4\left(\frac{\Delta A}{A}\right) +\frac{1}{3} \left(\frac{\Delta B}{B}\right) + \left(\frac{\Delta C}{C}\right) + \frac{3}{2} \left(\frac{\Delta D}{D}\right). \end{equation*}
[The relative error in a physical quantity raised to the power k is the k times the relative error in the individual quantity.]
