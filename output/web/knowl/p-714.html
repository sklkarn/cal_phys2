Bulk Modulus, \(B\text{:}\) It is the ratio of volumetric stress to the volumetric strain i.e., [Read Elasticity 1  for details.]
\begin{equation*} B=\frac{\Delta P}{\Delta V/V}. \end{equation*}
It defines the incompressibility of the material. It describes the elastic properties of a solid or fluid when it is under pressure on all surfaces. Its unit is Pascal (Pa).
