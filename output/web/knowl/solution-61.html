Solution 4.4.1.1.

Given: \(m=0.1\,kg, \quad A = 0.05 \,m,\quad f = 2\,Hz, \quad x=0.02\,m\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \omega =120 \,rpm= \frac{120\times 2\pi}{60}(rad/sec) = 12.566 \,rad/s\)
  2. \begin{equation*} \omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}} \end{equation*}
    \(\therefore\quad k= m\omega^{2} = 15.79 \,N/m\)
  3. \(\displaystyle A=5cm=0.05\,m\)
  4. \(\displaystyle E = \frac{1}{2}kA^{2} = = 0.0197 \,J\)
  5. \(\frac{1}{2}kA^{2} = \frac{1}{2}mv^{2} + \frac{1}{2}kx^{2}\)
    \begin{equation*} \therefore\quad v = 0.576 \,m/s \end{equation*}
  6. \begin{equation*} x = A\cos\omega t \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} \therefore\quad t = 0.293 \,s \end{equation*}
  7. \begin{equation*} F = -kx \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} \therefore\quad F = - 0.316 \,N \end{equation*}
  8. \begin{equation*} T = \frac{1}{f} \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} \therefore\quad T = 0.5 \,s \end{equation*}
