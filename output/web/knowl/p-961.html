The phenomenon of splitting of white light into its constituent colors when passes through a prism is known as dispersion and the image thus formed on a screen is called a spectrum. The spectrum consists of a visible and invisible regions of light. The principal colors of light in the visible spectrum are given as VIBGYOR (Violet, Indigo, Blue, Green, Yellow, Orange, and Red).
