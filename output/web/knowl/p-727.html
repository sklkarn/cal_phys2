But from Bulk modulus of elasticity,
\begin{equation*} B=-\frac{P}{\,dV/V_{i}} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad P = -B\left(\frac{\,dV}{V_{i}}\right) \end{equation*}
where
\begin{equation*} \,dV=V_{f}-V_{i} = A[(x_{2}+s_{2})-(x_{1}+s_{1})]-A\,dx \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = A[(x_{2}-x_{1})+(s_{2}-s_{1})]-A\,dx = A(\,dx+\,ds)-A\,dx =A\,ds \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore \quad P = -B\frac{A\,ds}{A\,dx}=-B\frac{\,ds}{\,dx} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation} \text{or,} \quad \frac{\,dP}{\,dx} = -B\frac{\,d^{2}s}{\,dx^{2}} \tag{4.4.2} \end{equation}
From eqns. (4.4.1) and (4.4.2), we have
\begin{equation*} -\rho \frac{\,d^{2}s}{\,dt^{2}}=-B\frac{\,d^{2}s}{\,dx^{2}} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation} \therefore \quad \frac{\,d^{2}s}{\,dx^{2}}=\frac{\rho}{B}\frac{\,d^{2}s}{\,dt^{2}}\tag{4.4.3} \end{equation}
Which is the wave equation of longitudinal wave. Hence
\begin{equation} s(x,t) = s_{o}\sin(kx-\omega t) \tag{4.4.4} \end{equation}
where \(s_{o} \) is the amplitude of the wave or maximum displacement of fluid column,
\begin{equation*} \omega =2\pi f \end{equation*}
is angular frequency, and
\begin{equation*} k=\frac{2\pi}{\lambda} \end{equation*}
is the wave number.
\begin{equation*} \text{Now}\quad \frac{\,ds(x,t)}{\,dx} =s_{o}k\cos(kx-\omega t) \end{equation*}
