A rainbow will form when the angle of deviation \(\phi\) is maximum for the given angle of incidence i, i.e., when \(\frac{\,d\phi}{\,di}=0\text{.}\) If this condition is satisfied, all the rays with incident angle close to \(i\) will be sent back in the same direction, producing a bright zone in the sky.
