The wave function of standing wave can be found as
\begin{equation*} y_{1}(x,t)=A\sin (kx-\omega t)\quad [\text{wave travels along +x axis.}] \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} y_{2}(x,t)=A\sin (kx+\omega t)\quad [\text{wave travels along -x axis.}] \end{equation*}
From the principle of superposition the resultant wave is
\begin{equation*} y(x,t)=y_{1}(x,t)+y_{2}(x,t) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = A\left[\sin (kx-\omega t)+\sin (kx+\omega t)\right] \end{equation*}
