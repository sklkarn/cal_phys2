Solution 5.7.4.1.

Given: Size of eye ball, \(a= 2.00 \,cm,\) size of object, \(y = 10 \,cm,\) distance of object from eye, \(d=200 \,cm,\) and size of image, \(b= ?\)
\begin{equation*} \tan\alpha = \frac{b}{a}=\frac{y}{d} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad b=\frac{ya}{d} = \frac{10 \,cm\times 2\,cm}{200 \,cm}= 1 \,mm \end{equation*}
