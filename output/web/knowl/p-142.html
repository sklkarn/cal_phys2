Geometric Formulae:
  1. Circumference of a circle of circle of radius r, \(C= 2\pi r\text{;}\)
  2. Area of a circle of circle of radius r, \(A = \pi r^{2};\)
  3. Volume of a sphere of radius r, \(V=\frac{4}{3}\pi r^{3};\)
  4. Surface area of a sphere of radius r, \(A=4\pi r^{2};\)
  5. Volume area of a cylinder of radius r and height h, \(V=\pi r^{2}h;\)
  6. Cylindrical surface area of a cylinder of radius r and height h, \(S=2\pi r h\)
  7. Congruent Triangles: In two triangles if corresponding SAS (side angle side) or SSS (side side side) are equal then triangles are congruent.
  8. Similar Triangles: If corresponding AAA (angle angle angle) of two triangles are equal then the triangles are called similar triangles. In that case the ratio of corresponding sides of these triangles are equal.
