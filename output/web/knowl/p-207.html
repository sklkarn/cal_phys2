Alternative
\begin{equation*} U=\frac{1}{2}CV^{2} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{but}\quad C=\frac{A\epsilon_{o}}{d} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{and}\quad E=\frac{V}{d} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad U=\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{A\epsilon_{o}}{d}\right)E^{2}d^{2} = \frac{1}{2}\epsilon_{o}E^{2}Ad = \frac{1}{2}\epsilon_{o}E^{2} \text{(Vol)} \end{equation*}
Hence
\begin{equation} u=\frac{U}{\text{(Vol)}}=\frac{1}{2}\epsilon_{o}E^{2}\tag{1.7.16} \end{equation}
