From lens formula,
\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{f} =\frac{1}{s} +\frac{1}{s'} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad \frac{1}{f} =\frac{1}{s} +\frac{1}{x-s} \end{equation*}
\([\because \quad x=s+s']\)
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad \frac{x-s+s}{s(x-s)} =\frac{1}{f} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad xf=xs-s^{2} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad s^{2}-sx+xf=0 \end{equation*}
