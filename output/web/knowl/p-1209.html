In a double slit interference experiment, the spacing between the slits is \(10 \,\mu m\) and the screen is at a distance of 2 m. If light of wavelength 500 nm is used, find
  1. the location of the first dark fringe,
  2. the location of the third (off axis) bright fringe,
  3. the spacing between the fringes,
  4. the theoretical maximum number of bright fringes possible.
