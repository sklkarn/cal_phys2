Exercise 40.

Two parallel wires are 100 m long and 10 cm apart and carry opposite charges of \(100 \,\mu C.\)
  1. What is the electrical force between the wires?
  2. How would this answer have changed if the wires had both been positively charged? Negatively charged?
  3. How would this answer have changed if the wires had been point charges located 10 cm apart?
  4. How would this answer have changed if the wires had been parallel sheets of metal 100 m long, 200 m wide, and 10 cm apart?
