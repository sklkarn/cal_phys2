A Tesla coil is constructed by wrapping \(N_{1}\) turns of wire along a length l of a hollow tube of cross-sectional area A and then wrapping a second coil \(N_{2}\) times around the first coil.
  1. What is the mutual inductance of the two coils?
  2. If \(N_{1} = 10,000\) turns, \(N_{2} = 100\) turns, \(l = 1 \,m,\) and the radius of the solenoid is 1 cm, what is the numerical value of the mutual inductance?
  3. If a radio frequency signal of \(1000 \,kHz\) is sent through the second coil such that the current oscillates with an amplitude of \(100 \,mA,\) what is the maximum average magnetic flux through each turn of the first coil?
  4. What is the maximum current through the first coil?
  5. What is the maximum induced emf in the first coil?
  6. What is the maximum induced back-emf in the first coil?
