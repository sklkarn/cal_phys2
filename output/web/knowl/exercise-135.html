Exercise 18.

A water wave demonstration tank is 30 m long. At one end, at the origin, Mary is generating waves described by the function
\begin{equation*} y_{1} = 2.0\,cm \cos [ \pi/(18 \,m) ( 4 x - (1 \,m/s) t ) ]. \end{equation*}
At the other end Paul is generating waves described by
\begin{equation*} y_{2} = 4.0\,cm \sin [ \pi/(6 \,s) ( ( 2 \,s/m ) x + 0.5 t ) ]. \end{equation*}
At \(x = 20\, m,\) Rita is recording the water level.
  1. What level does she record at t = 8 s?
  2. What frequency describes the data she records?
