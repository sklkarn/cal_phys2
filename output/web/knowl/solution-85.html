Solution 5.5.9.1.

Given: \(\lambda_{o} =5.00\times10^{-7}\, m; \quad n_{g} = 1.66 \quad n_{c} = 1.58 \)
  1. \begin{equation*} \lambda_{g}=\frac{\lambda_{o}}{n_{g}}\Rightarrow \lambda_{g} = 3.01\times10^{-7} \,m \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \lambda_{c}=\frac{\lambda_{o}}{n_{c}}\Rightarrow \lambda_{c} = 3.16\times10^{-7} \,m \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} 2d =\frac{\lambda_{c}}{2}\Rightarrow d = 7.91\times10^{-8} \,m. \end{equation*}
