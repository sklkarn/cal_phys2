Substituting the value of \(h_{2}\) in eqn. (5.2.33), we get -
\begin{equation*} \frac{h_{1}}{f} = \frac{h_{1}}{f_{1}}+\frac{h_{1}(f_{1}-d)}{f_{1}f_{2}} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{f_{1}}+\frac{1}{f_{2}}-\frac{d}{f_{1}f_{2}} \end{equation*}
