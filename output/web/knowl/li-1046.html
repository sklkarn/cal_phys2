Item b.

As I and II rays are \(180^{o}\) out of phase due to reflection from a surface of high refracting medium, so in order to make them interfere destructively total path difference between them must be equal to integral multiple of \(\lambda/2\)
\begin{equation*} \therefore 2t = m\frac{\lambda_{film}}{2} \end{equation*}
set m = 1$ for minimum thickness.
\begin{equation*} \text{or},\quad t = \frac{\lambda_{film}}{4} = \frac{\lambda_{o}}{4 n_{2}} = \frac{550\,nm}{4\times1.4} =98\,nm \end{equation*}
