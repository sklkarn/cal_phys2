This is a terrestrial telescope designed by Galileo. It consists of a convex lens as an objective and the concave lens as an eyepiece. The objective formed the image of an object at the other side of the concave lens. This image A’B’ formed at the second focus of the concave lens and behaves as a virtual object for concave lens.
