Since the displacement of particles are maximum at antinodes in standing wave we can set in eqn. (4.4.6) as
\begin{equation*} 2A\,\sin kx=2A \Rightarrow \sin kx= \pm1 = \sin (n+\frac{1}{2})\pi \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore \quad x= \frac{(n+\frac{1}{2})\pi}{k} = (n+\frac{1}{2})\frac{\lambda}{2} \qquad n=0,1,2,3,\cdots \end{equation*}
