Potential Gradient:
\begin{equation*} \Delta V = -\frac{W}{q}= -\frac{F\Delta l}{q}=-\frac{qE\Delta l}{q} = -E\Delta l \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,} \quad E = -\frac{\Delta V }{\Delta l} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation} \therefore\quad \vec{E} = - \vec{\nabla} V\tag{1.7.1} \end{equation}
Unit of potential gradient is V/m or N/C.
