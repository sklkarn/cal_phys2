If c is a velocity of light in a vacuum and v is the velocity of light in a given medium then the refractive index of this medium is defined as
\begin{equation} \eta = \frac{c}{v}\tag{5.2.5} \end{equation}
Medium with higher refractive index is known as optically denser medium and that with lower refractive index is known as optically rare medium.
