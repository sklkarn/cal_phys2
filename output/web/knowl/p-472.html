Two protons move together in the +x direction separated vertically by a distance r = 1 mm. If their speed is \(v = 1.00\times 10^{7} \,m/s,\) find
  1. the electrical force between them (is it attractive or repulsive),
  2. the magnetic force between them (is it attractive or repulsive),
  3. the total electromagnetic force between them (is it attractive or repulsive), and
  4. the ratio of electrical force to magnetic force. What do these answers become if v = 100 m/s?
