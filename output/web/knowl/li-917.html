Item 2.

The ratio of sine of the angles of incidence to the sine of angle of refraction is equal to the ratio of refractive indices of the second and first medium, i.e.
\begin{equation*} \frac{\sin \theta_{i}}{\sin \theta_{r}}= \frac{\eta_{r}}{\eta_{i}}= {}^{i}{\eta_{r}}. \end{equation*}
