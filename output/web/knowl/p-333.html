Applying Kirchhoff’s I law at junction A,
\begin{equation*} I=I_{1}+I_{2} \end{equation*}
Applying Kirchhoff’s II law to the loop ABDA,
\begin{equation} I_{1}P+I_{g}G-I_{2}R=0\tag{2.4.3} \end{equation}
Note: Here current cycle is taken along \(I_{1},\) so any other current following in this direction in the loop is taken as positive. Internal resistance of the galvanometer is considered as G.
