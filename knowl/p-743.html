Pitch, Notes and Tones: A pitch is the frequency of a sound that can be heard by a listener. The frequency of vibration of the vocal chord is perceived as the pitch of the voice. A note is a mixture of several frequencies of sound. It is pleasant to hear. A tone is a steady periodic sound characterized by timber or quality of a frequency. It is a single frequency sound. In physics pitch, notes, and tones all describe the frequency of vibration.
