Surface charge density, \(\sigma:\) If the charges are distributed uniformly on a surface area then the charge density is called a surface charge density. It is defined as the amount of charge per unit area. Let say the charge Q be distributed smoothly on a plane of area A, then
\begin{equation*} \sigma = \frac{Q}{A} \end{equation*}
