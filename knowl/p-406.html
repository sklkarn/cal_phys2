A small particle of mass 2 g and charge \(10 \,\mu C\) is emitted at \(t = 0\) from the origin with the velocity \(\vec{v} = 3 \,m/s \hat{i}+ 4 \,m/s \hat{k}\) in a uniform magnetic field of strength \(100 \,T \)in the z-direction. If there is no electrical field and no gravity,
  1. what is the force on the particle at t = 0?
  2. What is the magnitude and direction of the acceleration of the particle at t = 0?
  3. What is the radius of the helix about which the particle moves?
  4. How long does it take the particle to complete one revolution of its motion?
  5. How far along the z-direction has the particle moved during this revolution?
