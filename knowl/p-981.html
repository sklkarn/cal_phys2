Consider C and F are the crown and flint glass prisms of refracting angles A and A’, respectively. Let \(\eta_{b}\text{,}\) \(\eta\text{,}\) \(\eta_{r}\) and \(\eta'_{b}\text{,}\) \(\eta'\text{,}\) \(\eta'_{r} \) be the refractive indices of the two materials for blue, mean and red light. The angular dispersion for blue and red light produced by these prisms are given by
\begin{equation*} \delta_{b}-\delta_{r}=A(\eta_{b}-\eta_{r}) \qquad \text{for crown glass prism} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{and}\quad \delta'_{b}-\delta'_{r}=A'(\eta'_{b}-\eta'_{r}) \qquad \text{for flint glass prism} \end{equation*}
For no dispersion we should set
\begin{equation*} \left(\delta_{b}-\delta_{r}\right)+\left(\delta'_{b}-\delta'_{r}\right) =0 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation} \text{or,}\quad A\left(\eta_{b}-\eta_{r}\right)+A'\left(\eta'_{b}-\eta'_{r}\right) = 0 \tag{5.4.5} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} \text{or,}\quad \frac{A}{A'}=-\left(\frac{\eta'_{b}-\eta'_{r}}{\eta_{b}-\eta_{r}}\right)\tag{5.4.6} \end{equation}
The -ve sign indicates the refracting angles of the prisms are in opposite directions. The combination of two such prisms which satisfies eqn. (5.4.6) is called an achromatic combination and a beam of white light refracted through the combination will only be deviated and not dispersed. The angle of deviation produced by the two prisms for mean light is given by
\begin{equation*} \delta = A(\eta-1) \quad \text{and}\quad \delta' = A'(\eta'-1) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad A = \frac{\delta}{(\eta-1)} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{and}\quad A' = \frac{\delta'}{(\eta'-1)} \end{equation*}
Substituting these values of A and A’ in eqn. (5.4.5), we get -
\begin{equation*} \frac{\delta \left(\eta_{b}-\eta_{r}\right)}{(\eta-1)} +\frac{\delta' \left(\eta'_{b}-\eta'_{r}\right)}{(\eta'-1)} = 0 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad \omega\delta+\omega'\delta' =0 \end{equation*}
where
\begin{equation*} \omega=\frac{\left(\eta_{b}-\eta_{r}\right)}{(\eta-1)} \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} \omega'=\frac{\left(\eta'_{b}-\eta'_{r}\right)}{(\eta'-1)} \end{equation*}
are the dispersive powers of the materials of crown and flint glass prisms respectively.
