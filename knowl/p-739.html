Since the amplitude of standing wave is zero we can set in eqn. (4.4.7) as
\begin{equation*} 2A\,\sin kx=0 \Rightarrow \sin kx=0 =\sin n\pi \end{equation*}
\([\text{as}\quad 2A\neq 0\quad \text{and}\quad n=0,1,2,3,\cdots]\)
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad x=\frac{n\pi}{k}=\frac{n\lambda}{2} \end{equation*}
\(\because k=\frac{2\pi}{\lambda}\quad \text{and}\quad n=0,1,2,3,\cdots]\)
