Item iii.

Electric field due to charged plane,
\begin{equation} E = 2\pi\sigma k = \frac{2\pi\sigma}{4\pi\epsilon_{o}}= \frac{\sigma}{2\epsilon_{o}} = constant \tag{1.3.4} \end{equation}
and directed along perpendicular to the disc, ie., \(E\) is independent of the distance between the disc and point of consideration as long as the point is near to the plane.
