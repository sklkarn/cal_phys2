The magnifying power of the telescope in normal adjustment is
\begin{equation*} M=\frac{f_{o}}{f_{e}}. \end{equation*}
The erecting lens L is just there to erect the real image AB to A’B’. It has no contribution on magnification. The total length of the telescope is \(f_{o}+4f+f_{e}\text{.}\)
