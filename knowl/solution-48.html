Solution 3.6.2.1.

Given: \(W=0.1\,m, \quad L= 0.2\,m, \quad I = 2\,A \)
\begin{equation*} B= B_{ab}+B_{bc}+B_{cd}+B_{da} = 2B_{ab} + 2B_{bc} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} B_{ab} = \frac{\mu_{o}I}{4\pi}\frac{2a}{x\sqrt{(x^{2}+a^{2})}}; \quad a=W/2, \quad x=L/2 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} B_{bc} = \frac{\mu_{o}I}{4\pi}\frac{2a}{x\sqrt{(x^{2}+a^{2})}}; \quad a=L/2,\quad x=W/2 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} B= \frac{\mu_{o}I}{\pi W}\sqrt{5} = 1.79\times10^{-5}\,T \end{equation*}
From the right hand rule, \(\vec{B}\) is out of the paper.
