Trigonometric Identities: in any \(\vartriangle A' B' C'\) with sides \(a, \,b,\) and \(c\) and angles \(\alpha,\,\beta,\) and \(\gamma\)
  1. Law of sines:
    \begin{equation*} \frac{\sin\alpha}{a}=\frac{\sin\beta}{b}=\frac{\sin\gamma}{c} \end{equation*}
  2. Law of cosines:
    \begin{equation*} c^{2}=a^{2}+b^{2}-2ab\cos\gamma \end{equation*}
