  1. \begin{equation*} n_{1}\sin\theta_{1}=n_{2}\sin\theta_{2}\quad \text{air to glass} \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} n_{2}\sin\theta_{2}=n_{3}\sin\theta_{3}\quad \text{glass to water} \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} n_{1}\sin\theta_{1}=n_{3}\sin\theta_{3}\quad \text{air to water} \end{equation*}
    Since the air to glass to water result for \(\theta_{3}\) is the same as that for air to water, the glass has no effect on the refraction.
