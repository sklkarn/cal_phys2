A transverse sine wave is expressed in MKS units as
\begin{equation*} y(x,t) = 2 \sin 2\pi(\frac{x}{3}-\frac{t}{2}). \end{equation*}
Find
  1. its amplitude,
  2. its wave number,
  3. its frequency,
  4. its angular frequency,
  5. its phase velocity,
  6. the particle displacement at t = 1 s and x = 2 m,
  7. the particle velocity at that time and place,
  8. the acceleration of the particle at that time and place,
  9. the concavity of the wave at that time and place.
