\begin{equation*} \frac{BP}{c} = \frac{AP-t}{c}+\frac{t}{v} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad BP = AP-t + t\frac{c}{v} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad BP -AP = \eta t -t = (\eta -1)t \qquad [\because \frac{c}{v}= \eta] \end{equation*}
If P is the point originally occupied be the \(n^{th}\) fringes then the path difference
\begin{equation*} BP-AP =n\lambda \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore \quad BP -AP = (\eta -1)t =n\lambda \end{equation*}
Also the distance y through which the fringe is shifted is given by
\begin{equation*} y=\frac{n\lambda D}{d} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad (\eta -1)t = n\lambda = \frac{yd}{D} \end{equation*}
By knowing the value of fringe shifts of the central fringe y, the screen distance D, slits separation d, and refractive index \(\eta\) of the material of thin transparent plate, its thickness t can be evaluated.
