Linear charge density, \(\lambda\text{:}\) If the charges are distributed uniformly in a linear shape then the charge density is called a linear charge density. It is defined as the amount of charge per unit length. Let say the charge Q be distributed smoothly on a wire of length L, then
\begin{equation*} \lambda = \frac{Q}{L} \end{equation*}
