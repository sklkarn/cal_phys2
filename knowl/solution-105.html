Solution 5.9.3.1.

For destructive interference by reflected ray,
\begin{equation*} 2\eta t \cos r = n\lambda \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \Rightarrow t = \frac{n\lambda}{2 \eta \cos r} = \frac{1\times 5890}{2\times 1.5\times 0.5}=3926 \,\AA; \end{equation*}
for minimum thickness, set n=1.
