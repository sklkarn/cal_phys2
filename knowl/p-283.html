Number density, \(n\) is defined as number of charge carriers per unit volume of the conductor. It is different for different materials. Now from eqn. (2.0.2) the current density, \(J\) is given by
\begin{equation*} J = \frac{I}{A} = nev \end{equation*}
Its direction is along the direction of flow of positive charge.
