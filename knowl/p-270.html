A cylindrical capacitor has an inner radius of 1 mm, an outer radius of 2 mm, and a length of 4 m. If the voltage across the capacitor is 10 V, find
  1. the capacitance of the capacitor,
  2. the charge per unit length on the capacitor,
  3. the energy stored in the capacitor,
  4. the electric field mid-way between the cylinders,
  5. the energy density of the electric field mid-way between the capacitors,
  6. the average energy density of the electric field inside the capacitor.
