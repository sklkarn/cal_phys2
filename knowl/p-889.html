The phenomenon of bending of light ray as it goes from one medium to another is called a refraction. The velocity of light depends on the medium it is propagating which causes the refraction. The measure of an obstacle that a medium imposes on the velocity of light is known as refractive index of that medium. Hence the light ray bend as it goes from a medium of lower refractive index to the higher one or vice a versa.
