For no deviation, we should set
\begin{equation*} \delta+\delta'=0 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad A(\eta-1)+A'(\eta'-1)=0 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation} \text{or,}\quad \frac{A'}{A}= - \frac{(\eta-1)}{(\eta'-1)} \tag{5.3.9} \end{equation}
The -ve sign shows that the refracting angles of the two prisms are in opposite direction.
