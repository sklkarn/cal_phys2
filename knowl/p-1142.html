A converging lens is required to correct far sightedness, hence distant objects need to be imaged at most 73 cm from the eye. \(u=\infty,\quad v=-73\,cm \)
\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{f}= \frac{1}{u}+\frac{1}{v} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} or,\quad f=\frac{uv}{u+v} = \frac{25\times (-73)}{25-73} = 38 \,cm \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore \quad P = \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{0.38} =2.6 \quad diopter \end{equation*}
