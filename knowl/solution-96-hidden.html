\begin{equation*} v_{1}=-15 \,cm. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad \frac{1}{u_{1}} + \frac{1}{v_{1}} = \frac{1}{f} \quad \Rightarrow u_{1}= 17 \,cm \end{equation*}
Again, for the far point let it be \(u_{2}\) from eye, hence,
\begin{equation*} v_{2} =-(15+85) =-100 \,cm. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad \frac{1}{u_{2}} + \frac{1}{v_{2}} = \frac{1}{f} \quad \Rightarrow u_{2}= 500 \,cm \end{equation*}
Hence, the range of vision = 500-17= 483 cm.
