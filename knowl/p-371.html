  2. How much energy is converted into heat when this wire is connected to a 12 V battery for 60 s?
  3. What is the mass of the wire?
  4. How much does the temperature of the wire increase (assuming no heat loss to the surroundings)?
  5. How much does the resistance of the wire increase due to this heat?
