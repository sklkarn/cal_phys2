Consider a rod of length \(2a\) having non-linear charge density \(\lambda=a|y|\) so that
\begin{equation*} \begin{cases} |y| = +y \quad \text{for +ve y-axis} \\ \quad = -y \quad \text{for -ve y-axis.} \end{cases} \end{equation*}
Potential at point P due to charge element \(\,dq\) is
\begin{equation*} \,dV = \frac{k\,dq}{r}\quad \text{where}\quad r=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}. \end{equation*}
