An average value of emf over one half cycle is,
\begin{equation*} E_{av}=\frac{\int\limits_{0}^{T/2}E_{o}\sin\omega t\,dt}{\int\limits_{0}^{T/2}\,dt} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = \frac{E_{o}}{T/2}\left[\frac{-\cos\omega t}{\omega}\right]_{0}^{T/2} = \frac{2E_{o}}{\pi} \end{equation*}
\([\because \quad T = \frac{2\pi}{\omega}]\) Similarly, average alternating current over half cycle is
\begin{equation*} I_{av}=\frac{2I_{o}}{\pi} \end{equation*}
Hence an average over one cycle is zero.
