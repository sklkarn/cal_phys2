Exercise 5.

A carbon resistor is to be used as a thermometer. On a winter day when the temperature is freezing, its resistance is \(237.5 \,\Omega.\)
  1. What is the temperature on a hot summer day when the resistance is \(233.9 \,\Omega?\)
  2. What current flows through the resistor in the winter if the ohmmeter uses a 1.5 V battery?
  3. How much power does the resistor dissipate in the winter if it takes 5 seconds to measure its resistance?
NOTE: For carbon \(\alpha = -0.0005 \,/^{o}C\text{,}\) \(\rho = 3.5\times10^{-5} \,\Omega.m, \) \(D = 2.25 \,g/cm^{3}.\)
