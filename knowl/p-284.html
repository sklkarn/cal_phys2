Resistance, \(R\text{:}\) It is a restriction imposed on the motion of a current in a conductor. Motion of charge carriers (electrons) are not very smooth in a conductor as they bump onto the other electrons and positive charge residual atoms during their motion. Such restrictions (or, obstacles) in flow of electrons is measured as a resistance of the conductor in current flow. Its unit is Ohm (\(\Omega\)).
