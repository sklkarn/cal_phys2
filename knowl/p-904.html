Hence the apparent shift, d of the object to be appered is given by
\begin{equation} d=OI = CO-CI= CO-\frac{CO}{\eta_{b}} = t\left(1-\frac{1}{{}^a\eta_{b}}\right)\tag{5.2.10} \end{equation}
where \(CO = t\) is the real depth of the object and \({}^a \eta_{b}\) is a refractive index of medium b with respect to medium a.
