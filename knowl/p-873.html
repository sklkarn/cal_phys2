Since focus and center of curvature lie behind the convex mirror the following sign conventions will be helpful in analyzing the nature of image formation by the spherical mirrors.
  1. All distance will be measured from pole of the mirror;
  2. Radius of curvature and focal length of concave mirror are assumed to be positive and that for convex mirror are negative; and
  3. Distance for real object or real image is taken as positive and that for imaginary object or image is taken as negative.
