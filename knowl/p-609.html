Let emf of \(E=E_{o}e^{j\omega t}\) be applied to a circuit containing a resistor of resistance R, an inductor of inductance L, and a capacitor of capacitance C in series as shown in Figure 3.9.11.(a), then from Kirchhoff’s law, we have
\begin{equation*} E=V_{R}+V_{L}+V_{C}; \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad E=RI+j\omega LI + \frac{1}{j\omega C}I \end{equation*}
