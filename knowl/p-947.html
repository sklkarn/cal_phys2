For a concave lens, \(R_{1}\) is -ve, and \(R_{2}\) is +ve.
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad \frac{1}{f} = -(\eta - 1)\left(\frac{1}{R_{1}} + \frac{1}{R_{2}}\right) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad f= -\frac{R_{1}R_{2}}{(\eta -1)(R_{1}+R_{2})} \end{equation*}
If R is larger, then f is also larger for such a lens.
