Ampere’s law is an anlogous to Gauss’s law in electrostatics and is useful to determine magnetic field when the current distribution is symmetrical. Amperean equation always holds good whether the current distribution is symmetrical or not but we can only use the equation to determine B when symmetrical current distribution exists. Ampere’s law is a special case of Biot-Savart’s law. In order to apply Ampere’s Law.
