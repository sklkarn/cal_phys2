Electric Flux.

28.

A uniform electric field of strength \(E = 100 \,N/C\) is in the x, y - plane and directed above the +x - axis at an angle of \(30^{o}.\)
  1. What is the electric flux through a surface of area \(10 \,m^{2}\) whose normal is in the x-direction?
  2. What is the electric flux through the surface area \(\vec{A} = 5 m^{2} (\hat{i} + \hat{j} + \hat{k})?\)
  3. What is the electric flux through a circle of radius 0.10 m located in the y, z plane? Is this last answer positive or negative? Why?

29.

A uniform electric field
\begin{equation*} \vec{E} = 100 \,N/C \hat{i}+ 200 \,N/C \hat{k} \end{equation*}
is directed through a cube whose sides are of length 20 cm along the x, y, and z - axes. What is the electric flux through each of its faces?

30.

The electric flux through a surface in the x, y - plane is \(-100 \,N.m^{2}/C.\) If the electric field is \(100\, N/C\) and in the direction \(\hat{i} - \hat{j} + 2\hat{k},\)
  1. what is the area of the surface?
  2. What is the direction of the normal to the surface?

31.

An electric field is given by the equation
\begin{equation*} \vec{E} = ax\hat{i} + by\hat{j}, \end{equation*}
where \(a = 10 \,Nm/C \) and \(b = 20 \,Nm/C.\) What is the electric flux through a surface whose corners are at (0,0,0), (0,1m,0), (0,1m,2), and (0,0,2m)?
