\begin{equation} D=\delta+\delta' =A(\eta-1)+A'(\eta'-1) \tag{5.3.7} \end{equation}
But from eqn. (5.3.6), we have
\begin{equation*} A' = -A\left(\frac{\eta_{b}-\eta_{r}}{\eta'_{b}-\eta'_{r}}\right) \end{equation*}
Substituting the value of Aâ€™ in eqn. (5.3.7), we get -
\begin{equation*} D=A(\eta-1)-A\frac{(\eta_{b}-\eta_{r})(\eta'-1)}{(\eta'_{b}-\eta'_{r})} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{or,}\quad D=A(\eta-1)-A\frac{(\eta_{b}-\eta_{r})}{\omega'} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation} \therefore\quad D=A(\eta_{b}-\eta_{r})\left(\frac{1}{\omega}-\frac{1}{\omega'}\right)\tag{5.3.8} \end{equation}
