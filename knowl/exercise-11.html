Exercise 11.

A positive charge q is placed on the +y axis at y = a, and a negative charge -q is placed at y = -a. A point charge Q is located along the x-axis.
  1. What is the magnitude and direction of the net electrical force on charge Q?
  2. What does this become for x = 0?
  3. Graph the x-component of this force \(F_{x}\) versus x for \(-4a \lt x \lt 4a.\)
  4. Graph the y-component of this force \(F_{y}\) versus x for the same interval.
  5. Use your graph to estimate when the magnitude of F becomes a maximum. Approximately what is that maximum and for what value of x does it occur?
