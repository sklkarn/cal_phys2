Two small copper spheres, each having a mass of 50 g, are separated by 2.00 m.
  1. How many electrons does each sphere contain? (The atomic mass of copper is 63.5 g/mole, and its atomic number is 29.)
  2. How many electrons would have to be removed from one sphere and added to the other to cause an attractive force between the spheres of magnitude \(1.00\times 10^{4}\, N \text{?}\)
  3. What fraction of all the electrons in each sphere does this represent?
