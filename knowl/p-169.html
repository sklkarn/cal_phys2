A long straight coaxial cable consists of an inner conducting cylinder of radius an insulated from an outer coaxial cylinder of inner radius b and outer radius c. The inner cylinder has a uniform charge per unit length \(\lambda\) and the outer cylinder has no net charge. Find
  1. the electric field at a point between the cylinders a distance r from the axis,
  2. the electric field at a point outside the cylinders,
  3. the charge per unit length on the inner surface of the outer cylinder, and
  4. the charge per unit area on the outer surface of the outer cylinder.
  5. Make a plot of E versus r for \(0 \lt r \lt 2c.\)
